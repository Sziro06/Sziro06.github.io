About me
Hello! I am Hüseyin Özdemir, 17 year old student at Fyrstikkalleen collage in Oslo, Norway. I am born in Norway, with Turkish nationality. I really enjoy writing screenplays and editing what I have produced in school, in my spare time. We have a large TV studio at my school and in some projects I use the various parts of the studio. From time to time I set up the studio, change the lighting, check if the sound is good from the sound mixer and mix between cameras from the camera mixer. Take a look at some of mye projects.
