What means screenplay to me?

I really enjoy writing scripts. What I love about media is movies, and what I love about movies is the story and the dramaturgy. How a story can develop throughout a film is very fascinating to me. That's why I want to develop myself and have good expertise in this profession. In my spare time, I like to write scripts and expand on the story, so that it becomes an exciting script to read or an exciting film to produce.
